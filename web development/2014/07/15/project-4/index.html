About

This Chapter is organized and will be operated exclusively for educational and scientific purposes and in furtherance thereof: To promote an increased knowledge of the science, design development, construction, languages, software and applications of modern computing machinery;

To promote a greater interest in computing machinery and its applications; To provide a means of communication between persons having an interest in computing machinery; To provide assistance and advice for students seeking careers in computer science.
